Lachhiwala is a popular picnic spot located at Haridwar-Rishikesh Road, 22 kms from Dehradun. Surrounded by green forest. Lachhiwala now comes up as best outing destination and a family picnic spot near Dehradun.
We Welcomes You!! For Your Visit In Dehradun(Lachiwala)

Encircled between Sal trees, Lachhiwala provides a calm and fun loving environment for visitors. Children and elders find Lachhiwal equal enjoying. Apart from nature views, visitors like to enjoy fun bath in man made water pools here. A stream from Suswa River is collected in number of pools. Lachhiwala is also famous among trekkers and bird watchers due to its solitude environment. In search for pleasant atmosphere, Lachihiwala is often crowded in weekends and holidays. Tourists can visit Lachhiwal all round the year and Forest Rest House is available for accommodation.

"

Lachhiwal has fine connectivity with main road since it is located at walking distance from Dehradun-Haridwar Road. Lachhiwala is 22 km from Dehradun and 3 km from Doiwala. Regular buses are available from Dehradun, Rishikesh (35 km) and Haridwar (42 km)for Lachhiwala
